About Us

Bridging the gap between rigorous academic research and industrial engineering.

PrithviTech is a scientific, engineering, and sustainability consulting organisation driven by elite researchers, and industry professionals from India’s foremost top IITians. We operate at the high-impact intersection of advanced materials science, predictive computational modelling, environmental system designs (air, water, solid waste), specialising in transforming complex technical hurdles into data-driven decision and, commercially viable industrial solutions. By bridging the gap between rigorous academic research and industrial engineering, we empower best solution backed with research, analysis and proof of concept.

Our multidisciplinary technical expertise supports clients across the entire innovation and life cycle of developed technology. We uniquely combine computational intelligence—including Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), and multiscale modelling—with experimental rigor in polymer science, biomaterials development, advanced product design, and material characterisation. From initial material discovery and formulation design to process engineering, pilot-scale validation, and manufacturing scale-up, our integrated approach ensures that every solution is scientifically robust, economically viable, and industrially scalable.

At PrithviTech, sustainability is a powerful catalyst for innovation and market competitiveness, not a compliance checkbox. We seamlessly integrate core engineering with advanced environmental capabilities—offering Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), ESG advisory, sustainability reporting, and climate/air-quality solutions. Backed by our world-class research ecosystem, we deliver globally competitive R&D across the packaging, specialty chemicals, pharmaceutical, food processing, and advanced manufacturing sectors, helping industries transform raw ideas into scalable, sustainable industrial impact.
