About Us

Prithvitech is an ESG and sustainability consulting firm driven by science, data, and practical implementation. We are a team of experienced professionals with multidisciplinary expertise across environmental science, ESG advisory, climate action, circular economy, regulatory compliance, and sustainability analytics. Our work focuses on enabling organizations to embed sustainability into core business strategy while meeting evolving stakeholder and regulatory expectations.

The initiative is founded by Saumya Agrawal, a sustainability professional with a PhD in Environmental Science & Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay. She brings strong academic depth and applied research experience in environmental systems, sustainability modelling, life cycle assessment (LCA), waste and resource management, and data-driven decision-making.

Saumya has been closely associated with research, teaching, and applied sustainability projects at IIT Bombay, contributing to projects spanning environmental impact assessment, air and water systems analysis, circular economy solutions, and techno-economic evaluations. Her work reflects a strong integration of scientific rigor with real-world sustainability challenges faced by industry and institutions.

At Prithvitech, we combine technical credibility with strategic advisory, supporting organizations across sectors in ESG reporting, climate and carbon management, green waste solutions, and sustainable innovation. Our objective is to deliver credible, measurable, and future-ready sustainability outcomes aligned with global ESG frameworks and local regulatory requirements.
