Our Services

Comprehensive ESG & Sustainability Solutions

📊

ESG Strategy, Advisory & Reporting

Comprehensive ESG strategy development, stakeholder engagement, and reporting aligned with global frameworks

  • ESG Framework Design: Implementation of BRSR, GRI, SASB, TCFD, and ISSB frameworks tailored to your organization
  • Gap Analysis: Comprehensive assessment of current ESG practices against industry standards and stakeholder expectations
  • ESG Policy Development: Creation of robust policies and procedures for ESG governance and management
  • ESG Rating Support: Preparation and support for ESG ratings from agencies like MSCI, Sustainalytics, and CDP
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Development of stakeholder engagement strategies and materiality assessments
  • ESG Reporting: Annual sustainability report preparation and disclosure management
🌍

Climate Change, Carbon & Net-Zero Advisory

Climate risk assessment, carbon footprint analysis, and science-based decarbonization strategies

  • GHG Accounting: Comprehensive greenhouse gas inventory covering Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions
  • Carbon Footprint Analysis: Detailed carbon footprint measurement and benchmarking against industry peers
  • Net-Zero Roadmaps: Development of science-based targets and decarbonization pathways
  • Climate Risk Assessment: Physical and transition risk analysis aligned with TCFD recommendations
  • Carbon Offset Strategy: Advisory on carbon credits, offsets, and nature-based solutions
  • Climate Scenario Analysis: Forward-looking climate scenario modeling and stress testing
♻️

Circular Economy, Green Waste & Resource Management

Circular economy strategies, waste valorization, and resource efficiency optimization

  • Circular Economy Strategy: Development of circular business models and value chain optimization
  • Waste Management Plans: Sustainable waste management and zero-waste roadmap development
  • Waste Valorization: Identification of waste-to-value opportunities and industrial symbiosis
  • Resource Efficiency: Material flow analysis and resource productivity improvement
  • Extended Producer Responsibility: EPR compliance and take-back program design
  • Recycling & Recovery: Technology selection and implementation support for recycling initiatives
🔬

Environmental Impact, LCA & Sustainability Modelling

Life cycle assessment, environmental impact analysis, and data-driven sustainability evaluation

  • Product LCA: Cradle-to-gate and cradle-to-grave life cycle assessment of products
  • Process LCA: Environmental impact assessment of manufacturing and operational processes
  • Water Footprinting: Comprehensive water footprint analysis and water stewardship strategies
  • Air Quality Assessment: Air emissions inventory and atmospheric impact modeling
  • Environmental Product Declarations (EPD): Development and verification of EPDs
  • Sustainability Modelling: Advanced modeling for environmental performance optimization
⚖️

Regulatory, Compliance & Environmental Governance

Environmental compliance management, regulatory advisory, and governance frameworks

  • Environmental Compliance Audit: Comprehensive audit of environmental permits, licenses, and compliance status
  • Regulatory Advisory: Navigation of evolving environmental regulations and reporting requirements
  • ESG Governance Framework: Board-level ESG governance structure and committee formation
  • Risk Management: Environmental and social risk identification, assessment, and mitigation
  • Due Diligence: ESG due diligence for M&A transactions and investment decisions
  • Compliance Training: Capacity building and training programs for environmental compliance
🌱

Sustainable Materials, Innovation & Green Manufacturing

Sustainable material selection, green manufacturing practices, and eco-design strategies

  • Biodegradable Material Strategy: Selection and implementation of biodegradable and bio-based materials
  • Green Product Design: Eco-design principles and design for environment (DfE) support
  • Sustainable Sourcing: Supply chain sustainability assessment and responsible sourcing programs
  • Clean Technology: Innovation support for clean technologies and green manufacturing processes
  • Material Substitution: Identification of sustainable alternatives to hazardous or high-impact materials
  • Green Chemistry: Application of green chemistry principles in product and process development
