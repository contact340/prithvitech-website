Dr. Saumya Agrawal

Founder & Principal Consultant

PhD in Environmental Science & Engineering, IIT Bombay

Dr. Saumya Agrawal is a sustainability professional with a PhD in Environmental Science & Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay. She brings strong academic depth and applied research experience in environmental systems, sustainability modelling, life cycle assessment (LCA), waste and resource management, and data-driven decision-making.

Dr. Agrawal has been closely associated with research, teaching, and applied sustainability projects at IIT Bombay, contributing to projects spanning environmental impact assessment, air and water systems analysis, circular economy solutions, and techno-economic evaluations. Her work reflects a strong integration of scientific rigor with real-world sustainability challenges faced by industry and institutions.
